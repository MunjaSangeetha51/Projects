Our Story

Born from a Passion for
Perfect Coffee

Sitara Coffee was born in Chennai with one belief — that great coffee is a ritual, not just a drink. We source single-origin beans from the misty Nilgiris and Coorg hills, roasted in small batches to honour every note.

From the first sip of our signature filter kaapi to the last drop of a hand-pulled espresso, every cup is a journey through terroir, craft and care.

The Experience

More Than Just Coffee

We've designed every corner of Sitara to be a sanctuary for the senses.

🫘
Single Origin Beans

We work directly with estate farmers in the Nilgiris and Coorg. Each batch is traceable to its farm, elevation, and harvest season.

🔥
Live Roasting

Watch our roasters at work every morning. Small-batch roasting ensures peak freshness — no bean sits longer than 7 days.

🍵
South Indian Kaapi

Our filter kaapi is brewed with a custom chicory blend, pulled through a traditional dabara set. Authentic. Irreplaceable.

🧊
Cold Brew Lab

18-hour cold steeped in purified water. No heat. No shortcuts. Just the pure, sweet essence of the bean — served over hand-cut ice.

🥐
Artisan Pastries

Our kitchen wakes before dawn. Every croissant, cake, and toast is made in-house, from local ingredients, paired for your cup.

🎶
The Atmosphere

Curated jazz, ambient lighting, and slow-paced service. Sitara is built for lingering — bring a book, bring a friend, stay a while.
