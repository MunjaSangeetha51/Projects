Love of Coffee

Sitara Coffee was founded in 2018 with a simple dream — to bring world-class specialty coffee to the heart of Kadiri. We source our beans from the finest estates across Coorg, Chikmagalur, and beyond.

Every cup we serve is a promise — of quality, warmth, and a moment that belongs just to you.
